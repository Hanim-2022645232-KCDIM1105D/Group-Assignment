Services

Home > Services

We provide collaborate with creating agencies to coordinate the transfer of records. Communication will focus on identifying records eligible for transfer, and our staff will maintain an understanding of the overall quantity of records for each department. They will also manage the removal of records from the center according to disposal schedules.

Upon receiving records in the Record Centre, staff will ensure they are accompanied by completed transfer lists. Responsibilities include filing and organizing the records according to agreed procedures. Regular file reviews will be conducted by the staffs to certify ongoing organization.

In response to requests from creating office staff, our staff will promptly retrieve and provide specific records, following established procedures. The movement of records issued to user departments will be tracked by our staff, who will ensure the timely return of records within the designated timeframe.

Our staff will be responsible for identifying and preparing records eligible for transfer to archival institutions or disposal. This involves categorizing records, organizing them appropriately, and ensuring compliance with relevant guidelines and regulations.

Documents will be picked up at the creating agency's location by our staff. You don't have to worry about place or time. This service is facilitated using our transportation, ensuring secure handling of your materials. You won't have to worry about how far places are from one to another by using this service.
